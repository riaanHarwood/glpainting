About

Your local experts in residential painting, delivering top-quality interior finishes across Auckland.

At G&L Painting, we are dedicated to delivering high-quality interior painting, roof painting, plastering, and gib fixing. With over 22 years of experience and 400+ homes completed, we bring precision to every project. As a Dulux Master Painter and member of Master Painters NZ, we uphold top industry standards. Using premium Dulux products, we ensure a durable finish that protects and enhances your home. Trusted across Auckland, we're ready to bring the same expert service to your next project.
